Athanasios Kriemadis

Professor Kriemadis has worked in the private and public sectors both in the US (Motorola, Inc., University of New Mexico, Basketball Hall of Fame, Research & Pooling, Inc.) and in Greece. He is a certified Evaluator of the European Quality Award (EFQM Excellence Award) and Inspector of Quality Management Systems (ISO 9001: 2008) as well as a Certified Consultant Lean Six Sigma Black Belt.
