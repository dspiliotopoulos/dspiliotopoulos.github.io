The Lab Mission

The SQT lab mission is to be a scientific and reliable body producing primary research, study and development of scientific knowledge, as well as developing tools and applications for the needs of businesses, society, and the economy. In addition, it will cover the educational and research needs of the Department of Management Science and Technology of the University of Peloponnese. Main activities of the Laboratory include research and study in the following scientific topics: Management, Strategic Management, Leadership, Human Resources Management and Organizational Behaviour, Entrepreneurship and Innovation, Total Quality Management and Quality Management Systems, Human-Centered Technologies and applications and services to improve digital communication and human life such as (a) Data analysis related to customer and business needs, consumer behavior and related applications, (b) Design thinking for business application and user experience analysis for innovative products, (c) Management Information Systems and customer relationship management including complex interactions through social networks, and (d) digital systems to support innovation, collaboration and utilization of collective intelligence.

Members of the lab

Athanasios Kriemadis

Dr. Athanasios (Thanos) Kriemadis is Professor of Strategic Planning in the Department of Management Science and Technology, School of Economics and Technology at the University of Peloponnese. He is currently Deputy Head of the Department, President of the Innovation and Entrepreneurship Committee at the University of Peloponnese, and Director of the Laboratory “Strategic Management, Quality Management, and Human-centered Technologies” and former Deputy Rector of Financial Management and Infrastructure, member of the University Senate and the University Research Committee.

Dimitris Spiliotopoulos

Assist. Professor Dimitris Spiliotopoulos is a faculty member of the Department of Management Science and Technology, University of the Peloponnese, Greece. He has a PhD in Human-Computer Interaction, an MPhil in Management Information Systems, an MA in Linguistics, and a BSc in Computation. His research interests include HCI, data analysis, UX, recommender systems, design thinking, and interaction design. He has been involved in research projects in cybersecurity, intelligent communication, human factor design, and data analysis for more than 10 years. He has more than 100 publications in peer-review international journals and conference proceedings.

Haris Kariofyllas

Haris Kariofyllas belongs to the laboratory teaching staff, has worked in the financial department of private companies and for 20 years in secondary education as an economist where he served in positions of responsibility while he has worked in a number of study programs as an associate professor and assessor. He is a specialist statistical analyst while his research interests include business research and total quality management.

Despoina Giakomidou

Despoina Giakomidou is a PhD candidate in the Department of Management Science and Technology at the University of Peloponnese. Graduate in public administration and a postgraduate in entrepreneurship and governance. With strong organizing, communication skills and always committed to innovation and leadership. My personal objective is to contribute to the strategic reengineering, reorganization of public administration through digital entrepreneurship. More than 25 years of professional experience with a proven, successful track record in the Financial sector and Public Administration in Senior positions. Currently, Head of Department A’ of Societe Anonyme (S.A.) of Greek Business Registry (GEMI) at Athens Chamber of Commerce and Industry.
